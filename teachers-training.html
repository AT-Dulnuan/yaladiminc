YELADIM TEACHERS TRAINING

EDUCATORS ARE MOTIVATORS

Yeladim’s Teachers Training equips volunteer teachers to conduct the YVBS curriculum. Through the various theoretical and experiential training activities, volunteer teachers are able to embrace the goldy values that are to be taught to the children.

EMPOWERING TEACHERS PROGRAM

Investing on and empowering teachers is a priority of Yeladim. Teachers can only give what they have. God’s word is the basic foundation of every volunteer teacher of Yeladim. Out of the heart, the mouth speaks, and out of faith are of good works.

Yeladim Teachers Training focuses on teaching God’s word. Only by God’s word can all other skills taught during the training be empowered and made effective to teach children.

YELADIM

A non-stock, non-profit organization registered with the Securities and Exchanges Commission (SEC) in October 2007. Organized by a group of experienced community volunteers reaching out to families in economically challenged communities.
