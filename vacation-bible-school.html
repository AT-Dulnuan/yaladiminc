YELADIM VACATION BIBLE SCHOOL

MAKING THE MOST OUT OF EVERY CHILD

Yeladim takes pride in its Yeladim Vacation Bible School (YVBS) program. Which has a uniquely crafted curriculum, complete with exciting activities that the children enjoy and truly learn from.

Yeladim Vacation Bible School is a summer class program for children ages 5 to 12 years old in economically challenged communities and who are usually idle and spend most of their time playing in the streets during summer break.

YVBS SUMMER PROGRAM

The program was first conducted in Brgy. Matandang Balara in 2007 with the partnership of a day care center and a christian church in the area. Since then, it has been implemented in several other communities such as Daan Tubo and Caingin in Quezon City and a farming community in Pilila, Rizal.

The YVBS curriculum was designated to last for 12 days, with each class lasting for two and a half hours per day. Using a variety of educational practices, the children are taught simple, practical skills in problem-solving, leading to their development of an enterprising spirit.
