START OF A JOURNEY

Yeladim Inc. is a non-stock, non-profit, religious organization recognized by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in October 2017.

The organization was created by a group of experienced community volunteers in the field of reaching out to families in economically-challenged communities.

We aim to aid and assist these children in attaining primary education therefore giving them a competitive chance to hopefully help them reach their dreams which in turn will help their families become catalysts for progress and be productive members of our society.

OUR PROMISE TO UPHOLD YOUR GIVEN TRUST

Yeladim’s mission is to enable children in economically-challenged communities to become catalysts for progress through entrepreneurial learning rooted in biblical values. With your help to donate, volunteer, organize a fundraiser, take part in an event, and become a corporate partner, the organization will always recognize and appreciate the support.

OUR STORY

Yeladim Inc. was formed when our Yeladim Vacation Bible School (YVBS) Head Teacher, Dorothy Sabarez, met our former president, Zerilda Maria Cunanan, at one of their volunteer services in Daan Tubo, Quezon City.

Dorothy, who has a heart for teaching and Zerilda, who has a heart for children, decided to use their love for children and teaching to create an organization that focuses on teaching God’s word to children who live in economically challenged communities.

The organization was formally registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) in October 2017 as a non-profit organization.

Today, the organization’s mission and vision continue changing the lives of hundreds of children every year with the help of its volunteers, partners and sponsors.
