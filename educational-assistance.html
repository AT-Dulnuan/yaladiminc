EEDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE

A DREAM IN THEIR MIND IS OUR PURPOSE

The organization believes that education empowers children by opening a world of learning for economically challenged communities. Having education as a basic foundation in life for the children’s future, Yeladim aims to help these children through their educational assistance program.

OUR PROMISE TO UNPHOLD

The program’s target beneficiaries are the selected students from the YVBS classes. The children chosen are those with the least financial capability, also with the most enthusiasm for learning, and that has a deep motivation for their future. Children that are selected into the program shall be granted financial support for school materials, school food “baon” and transportation to help them finish their elementary education at the very least.

Program’s goal is to have these children with academic credentials to enable them to become independent, and productive individuals for their own future.

YELADIM

A non-stock, non-profit organization registered with the Securities and Exchanges Commission (SEC) in October 2007. Organized by a group of experienced community volunteers reaching out to families in economically challenged communities.
